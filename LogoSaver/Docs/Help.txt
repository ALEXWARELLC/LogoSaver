Help | LogoSaver (For those who need it)

1. Installation?
	If your reading this then you already have LogoSaver installed!
	LogoSaver is currently a standalone application meaning that there isn't any installation yet.
	This may change as LogoSaver grows. 

2. How do I add images?
	To add images, simply hit 'End' on your keyboard while running LogoSaver
	or double click 'ImageLoader.exe' and add your images that way.

3. How do I close LogoSaver?
	To close LogoSaver, hit 'Esc' on your keyboard, this will close LogoSaver without any issues. 
	If LogoSaver stops responding you can hit 'Alt + F4'.
